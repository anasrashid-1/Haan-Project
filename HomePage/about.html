Your daily adventure partners

HAAN is a personal care brand that wants to build a better future for the planet and humanity. Our aim is to celebrate the pleasure of self-care through a disruptive approach where sustainability and effectiveness meet style.

With practical, portable and attractively-designed personal care products, we aim to inspire people to live the life they want to live, creating unique moments and experiences to enjoy on their daily lives, weekend getaways or holidays.

Our core values focus on doing good for the people and the planet

When design meets sustainability

HAAN products are beautifully designed so that you won’t want to throw them away. To make it easier and also reduce the use of plastic in your personal care routines, Refills are key to our philosophy.

With them, you’ll be able to use your products over and over again, saving not only up to 89% of plastic but also money. Refilling is a new approach to create a lasting change for the planet.

Clean formula comes first

To ensure that all our products contain natural and clean ingredients, we work with local laboratories. When a formula is  clean, it means it doesn’t use any chemical or synthetic ingredient that could pose a risk to human health or to the environment.

Our cosmetics are not tested on animals nor include any animal ingredient  on it, which means we’re also vegan.

Shaping a better future for humanity

Every life begins with water, so we believe everyone deserves access to it.

Our purpose revolves around the Water Crisis. Every time you purchase HAAN you are contributing to this, as we support clean water projects in developing countries.

So far, we’ve built 17 clean water wells in Malawi, and 5 more are coming.
